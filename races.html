Races

Adrets

Adrets are considered a new race and originate from the Central Luminant. Unlike other races, Adrets do not have a distinct animal feature, and instead have tattoos on their face as a unique feature.


Canor

The race of Canor are also heavily favored by the Authority, though not nearly as much as the Adret. Canor have three distinct facial markings to choose from.


Capra

Capras are a descendant from goats, exhibiting various goat-like characteristics such as horns, rectangular pupils and even masks the same shape as a goats skull. Capra NPC's are rarely found in the Lumen of Deepwoken and their characteristics are unspecified.


Celtor

Celtor variants are based on the real life Mustelidae family, consisting of weasels, badgers, and otters, among others. Celtors are portrayed as rich, greedy and prideful. They formerly had a place to call home, the Celtorian City, but was sunken into The Depths.


Chrysid

The Chrysid people are a race related to moths, inheriting their compound eyes and feathery antennae. They are a rare race to see and carry close ties with the other races of Greathive despite their conflicting view on technology.


Etrean

Etreans are described as snake-like people, who commonly have pale skin, and slit eyes. They are the most common race, and can be found most commonly at Etris.


Felinor

One of the most distinct races in Lumen for their infamous cat ears, Felinor garner the most attention of all races in Deepwoken. They are a rare race with little social or political influence to their race, mainly varying with the individual themselves.


Ganymede

Ganymedes are a snail-based race in Deepwoken that appear with dark skin, abnormal eye figurations, and sometimes wearing their signature hat. Being one of the rarest races of Deepwoken, they are very gifted individuals and possess a deeper understanding of the deep than most.


Gremor

Gremors are a race similar to deer. They appear with greenish, blue or brown skin, with horns. "Deer-horned pariahs who lived a peaceful life as nomads before the tides came. Tolerant of extremes that many others would avoid."


Khan

Khans are a rare race with light skin, and a monkey-like appearance. Khans have a special crown on their forehead which they and they alone possess.


Tiran

Tiran is a rare race that has a bird motif. They have special circle-like markings on their face. "Solitary folk seldom seen below the clouds. Sharp and independent, though strongly protective of those who earn their respect."


Vesperian

Vesperians are described as a beetle themed race with organically grown masks and very dark skin. They are most commonly seen at Greathive Aratel and are mostly under ranked by fellow Ganymedes.
